Exploring Javascript Benchmarks

I recently explored the concept of a programming benchmark, a performance test that could be run accross multiple languages, platforms, or sets of parametric constraints in order to inform decision making. Particularly important for many businesses and design teams is to choose the correct technology/language at the onset of a design task. The decision of choosing the correct technology often considers how the performance of the code scales with memory/bandwidth growth. Linear scaling of such systems is usually desired. In my activity, I studied how the time required for memory storage scaled with the amount of memory.
