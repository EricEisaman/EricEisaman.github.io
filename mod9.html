Pseudo 3D with Ray Casting

In 1992, 21 year old John Carmack overcame the hardware limitations of early PC's through an innovative approach to faking 3D graphics which he called "ray casting". This post is to pay hommage to, and provide an instructional example of this computer graphics technique which gave birth to Wolfenstein 3D, the first ever person shooter video game.
